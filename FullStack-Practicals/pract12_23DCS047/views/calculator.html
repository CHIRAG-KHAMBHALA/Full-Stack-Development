💡 Tips for Young Mathematicians:

  • 🔢 You can use whole numbers like: 5, 10, 25
  • 🎯 You can also use decimals like: 2.5, 1.25, 10.5
  • ➕ Addition makes numbers bigger!
  • ➖ Subtraction makes numbers smaller!
  • ✖️ Multiplication is like adding many times!
  • ➗ Division splits numbers into equal parts!
